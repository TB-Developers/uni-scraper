    Kampong Cham

      University Overview

    Founded in 1995, Kampong Cham National School of Agriculture is a non-profit public higher education institution located in the large town of Kampong Cham (population range of 50,000-249,999 inhabitants). Officially accredited and/or recognized by the Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries, Cambodia, Kampong Cham National School of Agriculture (KCNSA) is a coeducational higher education institution. Kampong Cham National School of Agriculture (KCNSA) offers courses and programs leading to officially recognized higher education degrees in several areas of study.
